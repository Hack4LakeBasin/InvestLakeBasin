Small Scale farmers can access Finances through investors who are willing to invest based on the minimum annum reports given by farmers.

How it works

Farmers Sign Up

Small scale farmers sign up on the platform and upload their produce reports monthly for a minimum of 12 months to be eligible to access investors. Once eligible, the farmers can request the funds they require.

Investors Sieve through applications

The investors who have already signed up can sieve through the applications that investees have made and be able to see the records the farmers have uploaded and make a counter offer.

Small scale farmers Accept the counter offer

The farmer then goes through the various counter offers and chooses one that best suits his/her needs. After which the investor contacts the farmer and fund the farmer as stated in the counter offer. Farmer then uses the investment to grow their farming activities.
